About Raja Priya Group

A Hyderabad-based team built on one idea — every deal, every build and every brand deserves honesty first.

Our Story

Started with a simple promise

Raja Priya Group began with a straightforward frustration: too many real estate and construction deals in Hyderabad were built on unclear pricing and vague promises. We set out to be different — every plot verified, every construction cost itemised, every brand campaign measured against real results.

Today we work across four connected disciplines — real estate, construction, brand promotion and social media management — because we've seen how often a client's growth in one area depends on the other three moving together.

Our Mission

Make every transaction transparent

We give clients complete clarity on pricing, documentation and timelines, so decisions are made with confidence, not guesswork.

Our Vision

Be Hyderabad's most trusted growth partner

We want every client — landowner, homeowner or business — to think of us first when they're ready to build or grow something.
